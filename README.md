# Hello, WebVR Cube 🧊✨
Welcome to Hello, WebVR Cube , a streamlined VR experience designed to showcase the power of WebXR within a standalone Windows application. Whether you're a VR enthusiast or a developer looking for a reference for controller tracking, this app provides a clean and immersive environment to explore.

### 🌟 Key Features
- Immersive Environment: Step into a serene, low-poly forest world (powered by A-Frame) that provides a relaxing backdrop for your VR session.
- Real-time Device Tracking: A built-in Status Panel monitors your VR hardware in real-time. Know exactly when your left and right controllers are connected.
- Advanced Input Feedback: See your actions come to life! The app tracks and displays every button press, including:
  - Triggers & Grips
  - Thumbsticks & Trackpads
  - A, B, X, and Y Buttons
- Interactive Objects: Engage with a dynamic 3D scene featuring animated shapes and realistic shadows.
- Standalone Experience: No browser needed. This is a fully packaged Electron app optimized for Windows.
### 🎮 How to Play
1. Launch the Hello WebVR Cube.exe .
2. Ensure your VR headset (Oculus, Vive, Index, etc.) is connected and active.
3. Click the "Enter VR" button in the bottom-right corner.
4. Look at the Status Panel in front of you to see your controller data and inputs as you press them!
### 🛠️ Technical Details
- Engine: A-Frame (WebXR Framework)
- Platform: Windows (Portable Executable)
- Version: 1.1.0
- Framework: Electron (with WebXR hardware acceleration enabled)
### 📦 What's Included?
A single, portable .exe file. No installation required—just download, run, and dive into VR!
